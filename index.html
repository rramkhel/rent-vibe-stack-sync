About This Project

Rental search is stuck in 2010. Renters scroll through hundreds of identical listings, filtering by beds, baths, and priceâ€”but those filters don't capture what actually matters. The listings that convert? They match how someone wants to live, not just what they can afford.

RentVibes is an experiment: what if we skipped the 47 dropdown menus and asked 5 weird questions instead? What if we matched renters to listings based on personality and explained why each one fits?
