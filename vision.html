The vision, the strategy, the why.

The Problem

Renters scroll through 200 identical listings. "Granite countertops." "Walking distance to transit." Everyone filters by beds and baths, but nobody knows what they actually want until they feel it.

Current rental search is optimized for inventory, not intent. The listings that convert? They match how someone wants to live, not just what they can afford.

The Experiment

What if we skipped the 47 dropdown menus and asked 5 weird questions instead?

RentVibes matches renters to listings based on personality and lifestyle, then explains why each listing fits. No square footage. No parking filters. Just vibes.

The Unfair Advantage

We're not a startup in a garage. RentSync powers 5M+ monthly renters across Rentals.ca, RentFaster, and 4 other platforms.

Real listings. Real intent data. Real distribution if this works.

What We're Testing

  • Do "vibe explanations" increase listing engagement?
  • Can personality questions predict which listings convert?
  • Will renters trust a search that doesn't ask about bathrooms?

The Team

Built by the RentSync product and innovation team in Toronto. We've been doing rental search for years. This is us trying something new.
