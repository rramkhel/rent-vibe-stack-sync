The problem, the hypothesis, and why we think this might actually work.

The Problem

Renters scroll through 200 identical listings. "Granite countertops." "Walking distance to transit." Everyone filters by beds, baths, and price‚Äîbut those 200 "matching" listings all look the same.

The dirty secret: renters don't know what they want until they feel it. Current rental search is optimized for inventory, not intent. The listings that actually convert? They match how someone wants to live, not just what they can technically afford.

The Hypothesis

What if we skipped the 47 dropdown menus and asked 5 weird questions instead?

RentVibes matches renters to listings based on personality and lifestyle, then explains why each listing fits. No square footage. No parking filters. Just vibes.

The bet: "vibe explanations" create emotional connection. Emotional connection drives engagement. Engagement drives leads.

Why Us

We're not a startup in a garage. RentSync powers 5M+ monthly renters across Rentals.ca, RentFaster, Zumper Canada, and 3 other platforms.

Real listings. Real intent data. Real distribution if this works.

What We're Testing

  • Do "vibe explanations" increase time-on-listing?
  • Can personality questions predict which listings get leads?
  • Will renters trust a search that doesn't ask about bathrooms?
